Partners

Local Farms & Co-ops

Our coffee beans are sourced from a network of local farms and cooperatives that uphold sustainable farming practices. By partnering with small-scale farmers, we ensure that each bean is cultivated with care and respect for the environment. This collaboration also supports the livelihoods of these farmers, fostering a community of quality-driven producers who share our passion for exceptional coffee.

Artisan Roasters:

We collaborate with skilled artisan roasters who meticulously craft each roast to bring out the unique flavors and aromas of our coffee beans. These roasters share our dedication to quality and innovation, using time-honored techniques and modern technology to deliver the perfect roast every time. Our partnership with these experts allows us to offer a diverse range of coffee blends and single-origin selections, each with its own distinct character and story.
